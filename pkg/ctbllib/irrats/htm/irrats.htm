Irrationalities and Conway polynomials

Appendix 1 of the ATLAS of Brauer Characters lists, for several primes p, common irrationalities which occur in p-modular tables in the book, together with their images under the reduction to finite fields in characteristic p. The exact format is described in the Introduction and in Appendix 1 of the ATLAS of Brauer Characters,

Extended versions of these lists, in the same format, are available via the links below, in different files for different characteristics.
